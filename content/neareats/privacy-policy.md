This privacy policy describes how NearEats ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use our iOS application. NearEats helps you discover nearby restaurants and cafes. It has no user accounts, and we never ask for your name, email, or any other personal identifier inside the app.

Information We Do Not Collect
-----------------------------

NearEats has no sign-up or login. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or any directly identifying personal information.

Location
--------

With your permission, NearEats uses your device location to find venues near you. Your location is used to build search queries that are sent to the mapping services described below so the app can return nearby restaurants and cafes. We do not store your location on any server we control, and we do not use it to build a profile of you or to track your movements over time. You can deny or revoke location access at any time in the iOS Settings app; the app will then let you search a chosen area instead.

Third-Party Services
--------------------

To find venues, NearEats sends the geographic coordinates you are searching to the following services. No personal identifiers are attached to these requests.

- **OpenStreetMap (Overpass API)** — the primary source of venue data (name, cuisine, opening hours, address, and amenity details). Requests include the area being searched. See the OpenStreetMap Foundation privacy policy for how they handle request data.
- **Apple Maps (MapKit)** — used to fill gaps in venue data and addresses. Searches are handled by Apple under Apple's privacy policy.

We do not share information with advertising networks or data brokers, and we do not sell your data.

Analytics
---------

NearEats uses **PostHog** for anonymous product analytics, hosted on EU-based servers (eu.i.posthog.com), to understand how the app is used and to improve it. This includes:

- **Usage events** — for example, when the app becomes active, onboarding steps, when the paywall is shown, and when a purchase completes. These events are not linked to your identity.
- **A randomly generated, anonymous device identifier** — used to group events from the same installation. It is not linked to you personally and is not used to track you across other apps or websites.
- **Session Replay** — anonymized recordings of app sessions that help us diagnose usability issues. Text and input fields are masked by default.

This analytics data is **not** used for advertising or cross-app tracking. NearEats does not request App Tracking Transparency permission because it does not track you in that sense.

**Opting out:** Analytics is on by default, but you can turn it off at any time from within the app's settings. When you opt out, the app stops sending any analytics events and session recordings.

Data Stored on Your Device
--------------------------

Venue results are cached locally on your device (for up to 30 days) so the app loads faster and uses less network data. Your search-type preference and your analytics opt-out choice are also stored locally. This on-device data stays on your device.

Children's Privacy
------------------

NearEats is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them. The anonymous analytics described above are not tied to any individual's identity.

Your Rights
-----------

Because the app uses no accounts and collects no directly identifying personal information, there is no personal profile for us to retrieve, correct, or delete. You can stop all analytics collection at any time using the in-app opt-out, and you can clear locally cached data by deleting and reinstalling the app. If you are in the EU/EEA and have questions about your rights under the GDPR, contact us using the details below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
------------------------------

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy here and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
